> Review with counsel. EasyCasa operates as a **licensed real-estate mediator**

> EasyCasa è un'agenzia di mediazione immobiliare regolarmente iscritta. I

> dovuta una **provvigione** secondo le condizioni indicate. Inviando la
> richiesta accetti l'[Informativa privacy] e prendi visione delle condizioni di
> mediazione. — *[Confirm exact wording, fee terms, and REA number with counsel.]*

**Why gated:** aligns with the Phase 28 messaging decision (à-la-carte + success
fee, not "zero commission"). The acknowledgment is recorded with a policy version
so the disclosure shown is auditable.

fee schedule, withdrawal rights, and any consumer-protection disclosures required
at the point a mandate/order is signed (owner-transaction scope, later phase).
